Template (Nunjucks) for rendering every synced Raindrop.io highlights & annotations.

Available variables to use

Article Metadata
  • {{is_new_article}} - New file indicator
  • {{id}} - Article identifier
  • {{title}} - Title
  • {{excerpt}} - Article excerpt
  • {{link}} - Link to source
  • {{highlights}} - List of your Highlights
Highlight
  • {{id}} - Highlight identifier
  • {{text}} - Text
  • {{color}} - Highlight color
  • {{created}} - Created on
  • {{lastUpdate}} - Updated on
  • {{note}} - Annotation
